## Tips

1. Generate 4+ variations of each prompt, pick the best
2. Use "vector illustration" or "flat design" for cleaner results
3. Add "white background" for easy editing
4. Say "no text" if the AI mangles letters, add text later in Canva/Figma
5. Upscale final choices for print quality
